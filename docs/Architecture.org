* Data Architecture


** DataManager

Orchestrator, lifecycle and policy

What does DataManager do?

*** What instruments do we support?
The 7 major currency pairs
*** What date range do we require? (Yesterday - 2008)
*** What dates (sources) are we missing?
*** What derived candles are missing (or stale)?
*** What order should data be built
*** How do we reconcile ticks -> M1 -> H1 -> D1

DataManager does

*** Enumerate required work

Identify the data we need and what's missing.

*** call download/acquisition

Call downloader to download raw Dukascopy data.

*** call decoders

Translate from raw compressed format .bi5 (lzh) current Tick format
into candles. 

*** Aggregation

**** Consume: external raw data: Dukascopy  ( -> dukas)
**** Convert: raw data to internal Candle   (dukas -> candle.m1)
**** Aggregate: Candle -> Candle (eg. tick -> m1, m1 -> h1, h1 - d1)
**** Store: save the CandleSet to file in .csv form
**** TODO Index: develop a path manager to ensure directory structure.
**** TODO Validate existing data and report missing data


DataManager should *NOT* 

*** Know anything about fileformats .csv, .bi5, etc.
*** No candle math
