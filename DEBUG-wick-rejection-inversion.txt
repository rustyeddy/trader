ALSO FLAGGED (separate issue, real, but NOT this bug)
-------------------------------------------------------
evaluate() does float64 math on price-derived values (fixed-point Price
cast to float64 for ratio division). Doc comment argues the scale cancels
out and is locally safe -- probably true precision-wise, but still a
violation of the project's fixed-point-only discipline, and that
discipline usually exists for cross-build/platform determinism, not just
rounding safety. Should be rewritten as integer cross-multiplication
(avoid division/float entirely) regardless of the inversion bug's outcome.
Ruled out as the CAUSE of the inversion: float64 imprecision would produce
a small number of borderline flips near the threshold, symmetric in both
directions. What we see is large (173 net) and one-directional. Wrong
shape for a precision bug.
