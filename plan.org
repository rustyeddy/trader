* Trader - The Plan

The plan is to build an automated trading system that makes money. The
elements to do this are:

* Ingest historic data
** Translate incoming into internal (normalized) format
*** Acquire meta data
Instrument, time frame (1M, 1H, 1D, etc) and date range (e.g. 2023)
*** Grok incoming format
**** Dukascopy
**** OandA
*** Convert to our ~normalized~ format
**** OHLC int32
**** CandleSet is a range of OHLC
The range can be seconds to days.
*** Save converted version of data
